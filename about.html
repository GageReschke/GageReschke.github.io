What drives me

Engineering for me isn't just a career path — it's how I naturally approach things. I like figuring out how stuff works, building things that didn't exist before, and iterating until they do what they're supposed to.

Growing up in Brooklyn gave me a hands-on, just-figure-it-out attitude. Traveling the world gave me perspective. And games — of every kind — taught me that the best systems are the ones where every decision matters.
